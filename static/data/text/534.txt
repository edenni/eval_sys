 Pororo starts to read a book Crong sits beside Pororo.
 Pororo skips the middle of the book so as to finish it quickly.
 After Pororo reads the end of the book Pororo gives the book to Crong. Crong declines to get the book.
 Crong shakes the part of the book which pororo skips to show that Crong knows that Pororo skips the middle.
 Crong points the pages of the book to let Pororo know that Crong knows the skip.
