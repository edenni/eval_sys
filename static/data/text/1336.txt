 Pororo opens the door and enters into the house. Pororo calls the Crong and says I am home.
 As Pororo calls the Crong Crong gets out of the house. Then Pororo turns his head.
 As Crong tries to get out of the house Pororo asks the reason what are you doing there.
 Reply to Pororo's questions Crong just shakes his heads at the door.
 Pororo inquires something that Crong carries with his left hands. Crong suddenly hides the palette.
